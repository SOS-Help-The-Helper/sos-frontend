How SOS Works

Each layer builds on the last. The system compounds.

"A family's house floods in North Carolina..."
S
Signal
A signal enters
Maria texts: "My house flooded. I have two kids."
She texts from WhatsApp. Could be iMessage, Slack, web, or voice. The channel doesn't matter. SOS meets her where she is.
What the system captures: Location, household size, urgency, needs — extracted conversationally, not through forms.
I
Intelligence
The system decides
12 partners scored. Emergency RV selected. Score: 95.
The matching engine evaluates proximity, capability, urgency, capacity, and trust. A decision trace records exactly why this partner was chosen.
Auditable: "ERV selected: 3.1 mi away, housing capability, capacity for 4, trust score 88. Next best: Red Cross at 7.2 mi, score 74."
G
Graph
Connections form
Maria → matched to ERV. ERV → connected to Free Hot Meals.
The context graph records: Maria is now connected to ERV for housing. ERV refers Maria to Free Hot Meals for food. Two organizations that never coordinated before are now linked.
New connection: ERV and Free Hot Meals coordinated for the first time through SOS. That relationship persists for the next disaster.
N
Network
Trust is earned
ERV fulfilled in 45 minutes. Trust score: 88 → 93.
Trust isn't assumed — it's earned through outcomes. ERV responded fast and delivered. That data means they'll be matched first next time, for critical needs.
Not just ERV: Maria helped her neighbor after she was safe. Her trust score went up too. Everyone is a helper.
A
Adaptive
The state updates
ERV capacity drops from 6 RVs to 5. Food site has 43 meals left.
Real-time state across the whole network. Who has capacity. Where roads are blocked. Which shelters are full. The system adapts minute by minute.
No more guessing: The next match already knows ERV has one fewer unit. The food site's capacity updates as meals are served.
L
Learning
The system gets smarter
"Shelter matches within 5 miles fulfill 3× faster."
Every match, every outcome, every connection feeds the learning layer. Patterns emerge. Weights adjust. The next disaster starts with intelligence the last one didn't have.
The moat: A competitor can copy the architecture. They can't copy 10,000 decision traces with outcomes. The intelligence compounds.
Maria got shelter in 45 minutes. The system learned from it.
The next family will be matched even faster.
This is SIGNAL. It doesn't just coordinate — it compounds.
