SOS welcomes Donor Advised Funds (DAF)

If you’ve contributed to a Donor Advised Fund (DAF), you can recommend a grant to SOS.

How to grant from your DAF

  1. Log into your DAF account at your sponsoring organization.
  2. Search for SOS Global Inc., or enter our details directly.
  3. Recommend your grant, one-time or recurring.
  4. We’ll receive the funds directly and send you an acknowledgment.

SOS welcomes Qualified Charitable Distributions (QCD)

If you’re 70½ or older, a Qualified Charitable Distribution (QCD) lets you give directly from your IRA to SOS with zero tax.

Why a QCD

Unlike a regular donation, a QCD:

  • Doesn’t count as taxable income, even if you don’t itemize.
  • Counts toward your Required Minimum Distribution (RMD).
  • Is not affected by 2026’s new limits on charitable deductions.
  • Goes directly to SOS.

How to make a QCD

  1. Contact your IRA custodian. Call them or log into your account and request a Qualified Charitable Distribution.
  2. Designate SOS Global Inc. as the recipient.
  3. You’ll receive a written acknowledgment for your records.
