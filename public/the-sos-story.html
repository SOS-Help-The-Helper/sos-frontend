The SOS Story

Born in a hurricane.
Built on research.

When Hurricane Helene hit Western North Carolina, comms went down, missing-person reports scattered across insecure spreadsheets, and supplies piled up in the wrong places while neighbors went without.

We watched people help each other instinctively — clearing roads, delivering Starlink, triaging on front porches. We discovered that the instinct to help is always there. What's missing is infrastructure.

SOS grew out of that firsthand experience, the work of Open Source Medical Supplies, and lessons drawn from every major disaster since Katrina.
